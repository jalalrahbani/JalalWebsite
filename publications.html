Published Research

Inferring cellular contractile forces and work using deep morphology traction microscopy

Biophysical Journal, 2024
doi: 10.1016/j.bpj.2024.07.020

This research focuses on developing advanced microscopy techniques to analyze cellular contractile forces using deep learning approaches. The work contributes to understanding cellular mechanics and their role in biological processes.

CdGAP is a talin-binding protein and a target of TGF-β signaling that promotes HER2-positive breast cancer growth and metastasis

Cell Reports, 2023
doi.org/10.1016/j.celrep.2023.112936

This study investigates the role of CdGAP in breast cancer progression, particularly in HER2-positive subtypes. The research reveals novel mechanisms of cancer metastasis and potential therapeutic targets.

Polarity and mixed-mode oscillations may underlie different patterns of cellular migration

Scientific Reports, 2023
doi: 10.1038/s41598-023-31042-8

This work explores the fundamental mechanisms of cellular migration, examining how polarity and oscillatory patterns influence different migration strategies in biological systems.

Metabolic networks of the human gut microbiota

Microbiology Society Journal, 2020
doi: 10.1099/mic.0.000853

This research examines the complex metabolic interactions within the human gut microbiome, contributing to our understanding of microbial ecology and its impact on human health.
